Kit Options

This section presents the available kit options for the WSPR Beakon project, designed to simplify the acquisition process and ensure compatibility of all components.

Why Choose a Kit?​

Building a WSPR Beakon from individual components can be challenging due to:

  • Component sourcing from multiple suppliers
  • Compatibility verification between different parts
  • Shipping costs from various vendors
  • Quality assurance of individual components
  • Time investment in research and procurement

Our carefully curated kits solve these challenges by providing pre-selected, tested, and compatible components in convenient packages.

Available Kit Options​

Basic WSPR Beakon Kit​

The essential kit containing everything needed for a fully functional WSPR beacon transmitter.

Kit Contents:

  • Main PCB (professionally manufactured)
  • ESP32 Dev Kit C V4 (38 pins, Type C)
  • Si5351 RF Module
  • Rotary Encoder with cap
  • LCD Display (16x2 I2C)
  • All passive components (capacitors, resistors)
  • Connection cables (Dupont cables)
  • Female sockets and headers
  • GPS module (optional upgrade)

Enhanced Kit with 3D Case​

Complete kit including the basic components plus 3D printed enclosure and all mounting hardware.

Kit Contents:

  • Everything from Basic Kit

  • SO-239 panel mount connector

  • Assembly screws (M2.5 and M3)

What's Not Included​

All kits focus on the core electronics and mechanical components. The following items are not included and must be sourced separately:

  • Filters - Due to the variety of bands and personal preferences
  • Antennas - Highly dependent on installation requirements
  • External power supplies - Varies based on configuration needs
  • Coaxial cables - Length and type depend on installation
Filter Information

Filters must be built according to the specifications in the Filters section. Component values are provided, but individual calibration is required for optimal performance.

Frequently Asked Questions​

Q: Can I upgrade from a basic kit to an enhanced version later? A: Yes, upgrade components can be purchased separately.

Q: Do you provide assembly services? A: Currently, kits are sold for self-assembly only. Detailed instructions are provided.

Q: What if I need additional technical support? A: Comprehensive support is available via email and community forums.

Q: Are PCBs available separately? A: Yes, individual PCBs can be ordered for custom builds or replacements.

Q: Can I modify the kit contents? A: Standard kits have fixed contents, but custom configurations are available upon request.
